RINGS

There’s more to Mangata & Gallo than our iconic engagement rings—why not choose a ring for every finger? Mix gold, silver, platinum and diamond bands in a head-turning stack or style them side by side on each finger for maximum impact. Engrave a gold signet ring, usually worn on your pinky, for a hero piece that only gets better with time. Make your next bold statement a cocktail ring that gets everyone talking—colorful gemstones optional but highly recommended. When it comes to Mangata&Gallo rings, a full hand wins every time.

BRACELETS

It’s all in the wrist. Our collection of bracelets features classic silhouettes and designs that push the boundaries of the imagination. Express your maximalist side with a strong stack that can’t help but turn heads: a diamond tennis bracelet, chain link designs and polished bangles. If you want to make a powerful yet pared back statement, try wearing a wide cuff on each wrist. For a subtle approach, a few delicate styles in narrow widths always add up to something unexpected. When it comes to Mangata & Gallo bracelets, make your own style rules.

NECKLACES

Add a long necklace or colorful gemstones the next time you want simple drama without trying. When you want to go all out, wear a mix of metals, lengths and styles for a look that’s sure to strike up aconversation. From bold link chains to minimalist pendants, when it comes to Mangata & Gallo. necklaces, one is never enough.
